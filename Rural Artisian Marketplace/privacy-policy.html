Welcome to Our Privacy Policy

Rural Artisan Marketplace is dedicated to empowering rural artisans by connecting them with customers who value handmade products. Your privacy matters to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like name, email, and contact number
  • Address information for order communication
  • Language preferences and browsing behavior
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to improve our platform, connect customers with artisans, personalize user experience, process inquiries, and ensure smooth website functionality.

Third-Party Tools

Our website may use third-party services such as translation widgets, analytics tools, and hosting providers. These services follow their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also manage cookies through browser settings.
