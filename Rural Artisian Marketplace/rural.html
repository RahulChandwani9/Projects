About Our Marketplace

We are a rural artisan marketplace committed to preserving traditional crafts while creating sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities.

Across villages, generations of artisans continue to practice age-old skills — weaving, pottery, woodwork, hand embroidery, and natural crafts. Our platform connects these artisans directly with conscious buyers, ensuring fair pricing, visibility, and respect for their work.
